About Eco Hub

Eco Hub is a student-driven initiative dedicated to promoting sustainability and environmental awareness. Our platform empowers you to participate in green campaigns, join hands-on eco projects, and make a real difference in your community. Whether you’re passionate about tree planting, waste management, or simply want to learn more about living sustainably, Eco Hub is your go-to destination.

Our Mission

To inspire and enable students to take meaningful action for the environment through education, collaboration, and innovation.

What We Offer

  • Eco-campaigns and drives
  • Workshops and awareness sessions
  • Collaborative sustainability projects
  • Polls, quizzes, and interactive content
  • Community support and volunteering
